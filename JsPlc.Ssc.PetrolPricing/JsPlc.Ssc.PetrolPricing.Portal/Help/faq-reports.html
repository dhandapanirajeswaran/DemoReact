Price Reports

On the main Price Reports page you can choose one a report. Each report will take a few moments to load and will display a simple representation of the report data on screen.

You can also download an Excel copy of the report data using the button.

Due to the amount of data it may take a few moments to preview or download a particular report.

Competitor Sites Report

Report shows how many and which competitors face JS sites within a 0-30 minute drive time

View the Competitor Sites Report

Price Points Report

Report identifies the various retail prices of both diesel and unleaded for each brand and provides a market summary of how many brands have sites at each price point.

View the Price Points Report

National Average Report

Report shows the average price of diesel and unleaded for all brands and their variance to JS.

View the National Average Report

National Average 2 Report

Report produces the output for the tracking competitor tab in the PPL spreadsheet.

View the National Average 2 Report

Price Movement Report

Tracks retail prices of individual sites over a specified period.

View the Price Movement Report

Compliance Check Report

Report checks JS site compliance of fuel price changes from the day prior.

View the Compliance Report

Competitor Price Range Report

Report shows the average retails of all competitors by grade, their variance to JS and their price range.

View the Price Movement Report

Competitor Price Range By Company Report

Report shows the average retails sorted by ownership of all competitors by grade, their variance to JS and their price range.

View the Competitors Price Range Report

Quarterly Site Analysis Report

The Quarterly Site Analysis report shows which sites have been added, which have closed and which have changed ownership.

View the Quarterly Site Analysis Page

Last Site Prices Report

This reports shows the most recent (last) prices for all Sites (including Non-Sainsburys competitors).

View the Last Site Prices Report
